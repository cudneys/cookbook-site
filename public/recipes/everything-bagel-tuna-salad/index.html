Everything Bagel Tuna Salad

This Everything Bagel Tuna Salad gives the classic lunch a flavorful upgrade. Capers and everything bagel seasoning bring a salty punch that pairs with tuna.

Total 10 min
Serves 4 servings
Source EatingWell

Ingredients

Tap to check off as you go

Instructions

  1. 1
    Stir flaked drained tuna, ⅓ cup each mayonnaise, yogurt and chopped onion, ¼ cup chopped celery, the rinsed capers, 2 tablespoons everything bagel seasoning, 1 tablespoon mustard, 1 tablespoon lemon juice and ¼ teaspoon salt together in a medium bowl until well combined. Garnish with additional everything bagel seasoning, if desired. Recipe developed by Marianne Williams
